What is Winter Of Code(WoC)?

This winter MSTC club of daiict arrange winter of code for mostly first year student. which students participating will be given a list of projects along with what technologies they have to use. You have to work on that particular project under the guidance of your mentor for a period of 3 weeks. Students need to contact their mentor who will be guiding you through out the project. Our main aim behind organising this is to build development culture in DA-IICT by training the 1st yearites and providing them the required path.


Why should You Participate?

It gives you an amazing opportunity to learn and dive into coding under the mentorship of the best in our institute. Our list of projects gives you a chance to pick up and work on any topic you are enthusiastic about.Along with it, the best project in each category will be given cash prizes, certificates and goodies too.


How to Participate?

Select a set of projects in order of preference from the given list of projects. We will assign a project and mentor accordingly. You have to create a github account and share the username with us. Create a repository with the project name you have been assigned and make sure to commit all the changes you have developed throughout the coding phase. If you are not familiar with git, then check out - Hear
Most of the time you will be working and pushing the code online which will require you to have a computer and a working internet connection with you.


How to Contect your Mentor?

You all will be mailed with the list of your assigned mentors along with contact number. You can then contact them directly. We will also create a whatsapp group on which you can ask doubts. At​ the same​ time,​ we​ do​ hope​ that​ the​ participants​ be​ polite​ while​ making contact.​ One​ should​ realise​ that​ after ​all,​ the​ mentors​ might​ not​ always​ be available at​ the​ moment’s​ notice. Ideally​ one​ should​ wait​ for​ at least​ a​ day​ for​ the mentor’s​ reply.


How to Submit your Project?

You just need to make commits on your github repository and nothing else. Along with this you need to submit a report containing following things :-

1.  Name Of Project
2.  Mentor Assigened
3.  Your Initial Idea (What you plan to do. Do write if you haven’t implemented it due to toughness or lack of time.)
4.  Summary of your work.

Follow the same format for Mid Evaluation report too.


How Your Project will be evaluated?

Best project will be selected on the basis of your idea, difficulty of your project, technologies used and final design.
